Profession and hobby - interior design Ideal studies for anyone interested in working in the field of interior design and for anyone interested in working in this field as a designer. The studies are also suitable for people who are interested in designing their home and the homes of their friends and family members as well as for those who are interested in making a good living.

If you are debating between an interior design course and a degree in interior design, you should know what the goals of each of these tracks are and what you can earn in each of them.

The study contents in the courses are many and combine different bodies of knowledge. Among the topics studied are basics of drawing, engineering calculations, design styles, architecture, uses of colors and texture, human engineering, space division, 3D, and the list goes on.

The duration of the studies differs between the various institutions, depending on the scope of the material taught in them. In the short courses, a year and a half will separate the learners from the final exams, while in other cases the path may be longer, lasting between three and four years. In each of the cases, at the end of the studies, the fresh graduates receive a bachelor's degree in interior design, or at least a final certificate in the field, depending on the educational institution.

The employment options in interior design are certainly many, so that every graduate will be able to find the ideal position for him. Among the jobs offered in interior design: design for private clients, preservation of historic buildings, work in the construction and planning industry, design of furniture and products, construction of sets and displays, and so on.
