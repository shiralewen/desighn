Interior design is a field of specialization in architecture that deals with planning and changing interior spaces in buildings. This profession developed from the field of architecture, and until the 20th century it was a single profession. Unlike architecture, interior design does not deal with the design of entire buildings, constructions and urban contexts, but with the design of interior spaces in detail. The interior design refers to the needs of the person in the built spaces, in public buildings, such as a bank, hospital and library, and in private homes and provides an answer to his physical and mental needs, for example, the location and size of the different rooms in the house, lighting that suits different needs, and goes down to the details of the design of the kitchen cabinets.
